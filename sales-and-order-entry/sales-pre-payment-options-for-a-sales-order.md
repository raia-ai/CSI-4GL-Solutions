long_description: "This document provides step-by-step instructions for managing pre-payment options for a Sales Order within the 4GL Solutions / STEEL MANAGER system. It outlines the procedures for generating a Proforma Invoice or a similarly styled document for customer payment requests. The guide details two main scenarios: invoicing for the full 100% amount and invoicing for partial or pre-payment amounts. For partial payments, it explains how to use the OE31 transaction to access the 'Payment' tab on a sales order, enter the required payment amount, and update the order. It emphasizes that cumulative amounts, including any previously shipped and invoiced values, must be manually entered. The document also covers how to print the pro-forma invoice using the OE7BA transaction and how to handle subsequent payments by updating the total payment amount.
