PO7K - Open PO By Product - Excel
=================================

This function is used to display open purchase orders and download to Excel.

This report is available as an Excel download only.

1. Choose Purchasing » PO Reports » Open PO By Product - Excel [PO7K].

4. Enter or select the Buyer (defaults to the buyer assigned to the selected vendor).
5. Optionally, indicate if you want to include open POs with ASG, CRD, and/or POA statuses.
