OE7YF - Open Order Credit Caution
=================================

This function produces a credit caution report for open orders.

1. Choose Sales Order Entry » Order Entry Reports » Misc & Custom Reports » Custom Reports » Open Order Credit Caution [OE7YF].
3. Indicate how many Aging Days must be outstanding for invoices to be included (must be greater than zero).
