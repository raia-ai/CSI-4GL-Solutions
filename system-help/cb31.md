Entering Transactions Manually
==============================

This function is used to enter additional bank transactions that are reflected on the bank statement but not yet in .

You cannot make changes to a line that has been uploaded to the GL.

1. Choose General Ledger » Cash Book/Bank Rec » Cash Book Entry/Maintenance » Cash Book Entry/Maintenance [CB31].

4. Enter or select the Bank Transaction Type code.
5. Enter the Reference number (up to eight characters).
6. Enter or select the Date.
7. Enter any Remarks for the transaction.
8. Select Debit or Credit transaction.

When complete press F3 to exit the screen. The process bank details window will open.

1. To post check entries to GL enter “Y”, otherwise leave blank.
2. To close the bank for the period selected enter “Y”, otherwise leave blank.
3. Enter “Y” to confirm (or enter “/” to cancel).

G/L Allocation

1. Enter the GL account code.
2. Enter the dollar value to charge the general ledger account. Multiple accounts can be charged for one transaction (positive number only).

Transaction Detail

1. Enter the dollar tax amount to be added to the transaction in the Tax Amounts field. Up to three different taxes can be added; tax is based on country/state location.
2. Select Debit or Credit transaction.
3. Enter “Y” to confirm (or enter “/” to cancel).

You must now post your entries using CB41. Alternatively, you can enable the “Auto Transfer Bank Detail to Recon File” switch () to automatically post entries.
