OE7YU - Sales By Customer By Order Writer Year Comparison
=========================================================

This report lists sales by customer by order writer, comparing total sales for the entire previous fiscal year against total sales of the date range entered.

1. Choose Sales Order Entry » Order Entry Reports » Misc & Custom Reports » Custom Reports » Sales By Cust By Ord Wrt Year Comp [OE7YU].

4. Enter or select the Order Writer (defaults to all).
6. Enter or select a Date From and Date To (defaults to first day of current fiscal year).
7. Indicate if you want to Include Inactive customers (only enabled if “ALL” customers and “ALL” Order Writers are selected.
