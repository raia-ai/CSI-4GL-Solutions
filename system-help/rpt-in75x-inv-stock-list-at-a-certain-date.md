IN75X - Stock List At A Certain Date
====================================

[need description]

1. Choose Inventory Control » Inventory Reports » Stock List At A Certain Date [IN75X].
3. Enter or select the Product Lines you want to report on.
4. Enter or select the Date on which you want to check the stock levels.
