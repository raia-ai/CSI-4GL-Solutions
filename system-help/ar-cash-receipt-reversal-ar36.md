# Reversing a Cash Receipt

If necessary you can reverse a cash receipt after it has been posted.

1. Choose Accounts Receivable » Entry/Maintenance » Cash Receipt Reversal \[AR36].
2. Enter or select the Deposit number you want to reverse.
3. In the Auto Deposit Update field, indicate if you want to update the deposit and submit postings to the GL. If you enter “N”, you may then edit the deposit in the Cash Receipt entry screen and post the deposit.
