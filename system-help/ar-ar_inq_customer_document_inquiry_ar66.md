Customer Document Inquiry
=========================

This function lists all documents pertaining to a selected customer.

1. Choose Accounts Receivable » AR Inquiry » Customer Document Inquiry [AR66].
2. Select the Customer that you want to view documents for.
3. Double-click on a Document number to open the corresponding document.
