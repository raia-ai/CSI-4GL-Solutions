PO73 - Open PO By Product Line By Month
=======================================

This function is used to display outstanding purchase orders which have not been completely received, for selected warehouse and selected (or all) product lines.

1. Choose Purchasing » PO Reports » Open PO By Product Line By Month [PO73].
3. Enter or select one or more Product Lines.
4. Indicate if you want to Breakdown report data by grade. If you leave blank or enter “N”, the report will be summarized by product line.
