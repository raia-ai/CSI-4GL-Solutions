Accounts Payable Inquiry
========================

This function is used to look up accounts payable information.

1. Choose Accounts Payable » AP Inquiries » Accounts Payable Inquiry [AP68].
2. Enter or select Voucher number, or select check-box to choose all.
4. Enter vendor Invoice number.
5. Select whether to sort by: Voucher number, Invoice date, Due date or Open balance.
