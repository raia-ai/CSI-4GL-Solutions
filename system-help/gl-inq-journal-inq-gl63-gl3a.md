Viewing Journal Details
=======================

This function is used to inquire details of journal entries (posted or unposted).

Status: “UPD” =posted, “ASG”=unposted.

Regular Journal Entry Inquiry
-----------------------------

You can either search for a specific journal entry, or all journal entries within one or all fiscal periods.

1. Choose General Ledger » Journal Entry » Journal Inquiry [GL63].
2. Enter or select a singleJournal Number to look up.  
   or  
   Enter or select a Fiscal Period.  
   or  
   Leave both fields blank and press Tab to list all journal entries in all fiscal periods.
3. Press F4 on a journal line to view its details. In the Details screen, .

Recurring Journal Entries Inquiry
---------------------------------

You can search for details of a specific recurring journal entry or all recurring journal entries within a fiscal period.
