Cash Book Reports
=================

Cash Book Details
-----------------

This report lists all cash book entries for a selected fiscal period.

1. Choose General Ledger » Cash Book/Bank Rec » Cash Book Reports » Cash Book Detail [CB71].

4. Enter the Posting Date.

Bank Reconciliation
-------------------

This report lists all the cash book entries as a result of the reconciliation. This is the outstanding check list that shows items cleared in that fiscal period and everything that is still outstanding.

1. Choose General Ledger » Cash Book/Bank Rec » Cash Book Reports » Bank Reconciliation [CB72].

Creating a Cash Forecast Report
-------------------------------

This function produces a cash forecast report by currency based on the current bank balances, outstanding payable and outstanding receivables.

1. Choose General Ledger » Cash Book/Bank Rec » Cash Book Reports » Cash Forecast [CB73].
2. Enter or select Currency Code (defaults to all).
3. In the As At Date field, enter or select the date up to which you would like to analyze.
