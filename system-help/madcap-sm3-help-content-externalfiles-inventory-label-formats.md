long_description: "This document serves as a reference manual detailing the various inventory label formats used within the 4GL Solutions system, which is tailored for the metal industry. It provides sample formats categorized by dimension, such as 4x6\", 5x2\", and various metric sizes. Each sample is identified by a unique 'Label Code' (e.g., LRLBLLG2, LRLBLLG4) and illustrates the specific layout of data fields. These fields include critical tracking information like Quantity (in LB or FT), Pieces, Purchase Order Number, Vendor, Heat Number, Material specifications (e.g., 304L ASTM A-276), dimensions, and unique barcodes with corresponding tag numbers (e.g., L000907). The formats cater to different needs, including standard inventory labels, case labels, physical inventory count labels, and labels with specialized information like doglegs or mill/case data. This guide is essential for users to understand and select the correct label format for tracking materials like stainless steel round bars, pipes, plates, and aluminum tread brite throughout the supply chain."
