Updating the Check Register
===========================

This function is used to print a report of all un-posted checks (created from screen AP363 and AP3L) and update all relevant Accounts Payable, General Ledger and vendor files for the most recent check run - upon processing will post entries to GL.

1. Choose Accounts Payable » Disbursements Processing » Check Register and Update [AP37].
3. Enter “Y” to Sort By Vendor or leave blank to not sort by vendor.
