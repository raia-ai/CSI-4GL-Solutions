Account Inquiry By Fiscal Period
================================

This function is used to inquire summary (balances) or details (transactions) information by GL accounts within a specified fiscal period.

1. Choose General Ledger » GL Inquiry » Account Inquiry By Fiscal Period [GL65].
2. Enter or select Fiscal From and To date.
3. Enter Account Type: Assest, Liability, Equity, EXpense, Sale or Cost of goods.
4. Enter or select Account Number, full or partial, leave blank for all accounts.
5. Enter Account Name, partial for search or leave blank for all.
