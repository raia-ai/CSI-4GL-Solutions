OE7ZR - Bill of Lading
======================

This function is used to reprint/fax/email a bill of lading.

1. Choose Sales Order Entry » Order Entry Reprints » Bill of Lading [OE7ZR].
2. Select the Type of Entry (IV = Sales Invoice (display only), BI = Branch Invoice, or CI = Consignment Invoice), then enter or select the Document number.
