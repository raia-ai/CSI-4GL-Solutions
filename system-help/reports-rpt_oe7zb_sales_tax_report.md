OE7ZB - Sales Tax
=================

This function generates two reports: one displays sales tax liabilities, the second displays tax adjustments.

1. Choose Sales Order Entry » Order Entry Reports » Misc & Custom Reports » Custom Reports » Sales Tax  [OE7ZB].
3. Enter or select Salesperson code (defaults to all).
