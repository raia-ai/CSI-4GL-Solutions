OE7YS - Sales By Customer Contact
=================================

This function is used to generate a report listing Sales Invoices (does not include Credit/Debit notes) by Contact Name entered at time of creating work order.

1. Choose Miscellaneous » OE Functions » Sales By Customer Contact [OE7YS].
3. Enter or select a Customer number (defaults to all).
