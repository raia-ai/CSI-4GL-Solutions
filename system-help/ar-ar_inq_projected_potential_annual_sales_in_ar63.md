Projected/Potential Annual Sales
================================

This window lists customers who have non-zero amounts in each of the following: Fiscal Year Sales, Projected Budget data, and Potential Budget data. Data can be edited in AR1B or AR17.

Choose Accounts Receivable » AR Inquiry » Projected/Potential Annual Sales In [AR63].
