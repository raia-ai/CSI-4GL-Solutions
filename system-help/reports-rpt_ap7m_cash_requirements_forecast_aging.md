AP7M - Cash Requirements Forecast
=================================

This function produces a cash requirements forecast and aging report, within a 90 day date range.

1. Choose Accounts Payable » AP Reports » Cash Requirements Forecast  [AP7M].
3. Enter or select the starting date Date 1 and ending date Date 2 for the report (defaults to today's date for Date 1, 30 days from today for Date 2 and 30 days from Date 2 for Date 3.
4. Enter “Y” to show the aging in the functional currency (default is blank = no).
