OE7ZN - Invoice Costs
=====================

This report displays invoice costs for a date range.

1. Choose Sales Order Entry » Order Entry Reports » Misc & Custom Reports » Custom Reports » Invoice Costs [OE7ZN].
3. Enter or select a Salesperson (defaults to all).
