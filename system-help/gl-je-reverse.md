Reversing a Journal Entry
=========================

1. Choose General Ledger » Journal Entry » Journal Entry Maintenance [GL31].
2. Click Reversal.
3. Enter (or click … to find) the Journal No., and enter the Rev Date for the reversal journal entry to be created.
4. Click Save.

If you are already viewing a regular journal entry, you can create a reversal entry on the Header tab by selecting the Create Reversal? check box and entering the Reversal Date.  will create two journal entries: the original and the reversal. This does not apply when creating a recurring journal entry.
