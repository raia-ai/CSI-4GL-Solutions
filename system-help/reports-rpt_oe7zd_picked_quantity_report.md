OE7ZD - Used vs Picked Quantity
===============================

This report compares used and picked quantities.

1. Choose Sales Order Entry » Order Entry Reports » Misc & Custom Reports » Misc Reports » Used vs Picked Quantity  [OE7ZD].

4. Enter the acceptable Variance as a percentage (can include up to two decimal places; e.g. 10.25% would be entered as 10.25).
5. Enter the Document Status to report on: PRT = packing slip created, INV = invoiced, END = invoice posted, SHP = order shipped, POS = POS order, or ALL.
