# AR7B - Listing Customers w/Multi Ship Locations

This report displays a list of customers who have multiple ship-to addresses. Locations are maintained in the [Customer](https://4glsol.com/sm3-helpdocs/Content/Admin/Config/FM_FL_Util/FM_AR_Cust_Mtce_AR17.htm) file.

1. Choose Accounts Receivable » AR Reports » Customers w/Multi Ship Locations \[AR7B].
2. Select how/where the report should be output (press F4 to choose from a list). When you press Enter the report will generate.
