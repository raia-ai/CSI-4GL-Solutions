Cash Receipt Inquiry
====================

Use this function to search for and view posted and unposted cash receipts, what they were applied against and the operator who posted the deposit.

1. Choose Accounts Receivable » AR Inquiry » Cash Receipt Inquiry [AR62].
3. Select a Fiscal period to filter the list, or leave blank to search all periods.
4. The Total of Deposits field displays the total amount for the selected period; to view a breakdown of the deposits by payment type, press F4.

Individual deposits are displayed in the table, including their status in the Pst/Abt field: posted=“Y”, not posted=“blank”, aborted=“A”.

5. To view details of a particular deposit, press F4 in the Deposit Amount field.   
   You can then press F4 on the Applied Amount field for a payment to see what document(s) the payment was applied to.
