About Outside Processing
========================

Outside Processing module is used when material is sent out to third-party processors for additional processing, e.g. sending a coil out to be cut into sheets. The resulting material is either shipped direct to your customer or returned to your warehouse for further processing or shipping.

Outside Processing comprises three parts:

* Confirmation —
