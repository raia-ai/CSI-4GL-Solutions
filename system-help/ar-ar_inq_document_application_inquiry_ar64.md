Document Application Inquiry
============================

This inquiry finds deposit, write-off or adjustment amounts that have not been applied to outstanding AR balances.

1. Choose Accounts Receivable » AR Inquiry » Document Application Inquiry [AR64].
2. By default all Warehouses, Customers, and Types of Entry are included; if necessary, clear the All check box and select specific entities.
3. Enter the date range of unapplied amounts that you want to search.
4. Indicate if you want to include Plus, Minus and/or Zero balance documents.

Documents that match your criteria are displayed in the table, grouped by customer. In this table:  

* To view details of a particular deposit, press F4 in the + field.
* To view details of any applied amounts, press F4 in the Appl Amt field.
* To enter any remarks, press F4 in the R field.
* To view a breakdown of foreign amounts, press F9. Here you can press F4 in the Other Amount field to view any deductions.
